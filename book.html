The Premise

Most founder-led businesses don't fail from bad ideas.

They fail from broken structure underneath good ideas.

The product works. The team is talented. The revenue is real. But the business runs on the founder. Decisions stall when they're out. Cash flow is a mystery until the bank balance moves. Hiring is reactive. Pricing is guessed. Operations are duct tape.

From the outside it looks like a company. From the inside the founder knows the truth: it's a job that pays well and never sleeps.

What's Inside

The patterns we see in every diagnostic.

Built to Break breaks down what's actually happening inside founder-led businesses that look successful but can't scale. The hidden patterns. What causes them. What they cost in dollars, time, and freedom. And why most founders can't see them from the inside.

It's not theory. It's not motivation. It's a field guide.

Who It's For

Founders running between $500K and $10M.

If your business is working, but it can't run a day without you, this book is for you. If you've already read every operations book on the shelf and you're still stuck, this book is for you. If you're tired of being told what successful businesses look like and you want to know why yours doesn't feel like one, this book is for you.
